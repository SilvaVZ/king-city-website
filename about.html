About King City

Our Philosophy

King City was built on a simple principle: do things right. We operate with transparency, clean contracts, accurate metadata, and genuine commitment to artist growth. We're not trying to be a major label—we're building something better: a focused, professional operation where artists understand the business and own their future.

Why We Exist

Too many independent artists sign with labels or work with distributors and then lose visibility. They don't understand their splits, can't track their royalties, and receive vague quarterly statements. By the time they want to move their music elsewhere, ownership is unclear.

King City changes that. Every artist on our roster has a clear contract, detailed split sheets, and direct access to their data. We invest in systems so you can invest in your art.

What We Believe

  • Artists deserve clarity. Every deal is documented. Every split is tracked.
  • Publishing matters. We're building real infrastructure, not just distribution.
  • Systems scale. Clean operations now mean faster growth later.
  • Long-term relationships beat short-term gains. We're here to build with you.
  • Ownership is sacred. We protect artist rights and composition ownership.

Our Focus

King City Music is our active division. We work with select artists, manage distribution, handle metadata submissions, and maintain a clean royalty system. We're small by design—quality over quantity.

King City Publishing is in development. When launched, it will handle composition registration, publishing administration, and songwriter partnerships. This is where independent artists reclaim publishing income.

What Sets Us Apart

We're not a distributor. We're a label. We're invested in your releases, your growth, and your long-term success. We maintain artist contracts, split sheets, and royalty tracking as permanent records. We use proper copyright metadata fields. We understand rights clearance and sample licensing. And we're building toward publishing, where the real money lives.

If you're serious about building a music career, King City is built for you.
